BraiNets is a research team of the Institut de Neurosciences de la Timone (Marseille, France) created in January 2020 focusing on cognitive computational neuroscience. The main axes of research are: - Neurocomputational bases of human learning - Brain networks analysis and functional connectivity - Models, machine learning
