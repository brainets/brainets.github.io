
Brain Networks and Learning (BraiNets)
======================================

BraiNets is a novel research team of the `Institut de Neurosciences de la Timone <http://www.int.univ-amu.fr/>`_ (INT, Marseille) focusing on cognitive computational neuroscience. 
The main axes of research are:
- Neurocomputational bases of human learning
- Brain networks analysis and functional connectivity
- Models, machine learning
