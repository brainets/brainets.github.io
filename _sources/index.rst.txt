
Brain Networks and Learning (BraiNets)
======================================

BraiNets is a novel research team of the Institut de Neurosciences de la Timone (INT, Marseille) focusing on cognitive computational neuroscience. 
The main axes of research are
    1) Neurocomputational bases of human learning
    2) Brain networks analysis and functional connectivity
    3) Models, machine learning
